Document Head ColumnsÂ¶

Data entries in the document head occur once per transaction. Some entries may contain multiple values. Columns are defined in the table following.

REQ SM COLUMN NAME TYPE UC VALIDATE DEFAULT JUST NOTE
– – – – – – – – – – – – Keys
M S TYPE LC U Document Type codes   L4
M S IDENTIFIER SK U     L12
M S REVISION S U   00 L4
– – – – – – – – – – – – Posting & Reference
M S STATUS LC U Document Status Codes   L2  
M S DATE/TIME D U   TODAY R10  
M S ACCOUNT A U VT=ACCOUNTS   L12  
O S BUILDING A U VT=CONTACTS*B   L4  
P AM REFERENCECODE LC U Document Reference Codes   L4
P AM REFERENCE A       L20  
– – – – – – – – – – – – Transportation
P AM ADDRESSCODE LC U Contacts type codes ST,SF L4
P AM ADDRESS A U VT=CONTACTS*ST,SF,PF   L12  
O S CONTACT A       L20  
O S CARRIERCODE A U VT=CONTACTS*CA   L8  
O S ROUTE A       T30  
O S FREIGHTPAY LC U Freight Payment codes PP L4  
O S TRANSPORTMETHOD LC U Transportation Methods M L4  
O S LOADINGOPTION LC U Loading Option Codes 01 L4  
P AM VEHICLECODE LC U Vehicle Equipment codes   L4  
P AM VEHICLE A U     T20  
– – – – – – – – – – – – Qualitative Values
P AM DATECODE LC U Date/Time Qualifier codes   L4  
P AM DATETIME DT U     R20  
P AM TEMPERATURECODE LC U Temperature codes   L4  
P AM TEMPERATURE N       R6  
P AM QUANTITYCODE LC U Quantity codes   L4  
P AM QUANTITY N.4       R12  
– – – – – – – – – – – – Notes and Messages

P AM NOTE A       T40  
P AM NOTEAPPLY LC U Document Type codes   L4
